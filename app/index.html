About me

I am the Director of Data Science for Asia @ H2O.ai, I head the customer data science team and am responsible for AI product innovation. I wear multiple hats, providing leadership and support across various verticals including product development, research and innovation, and customer facing interactions.

Over a decade of experience in the data science industry. My experience is both broad and deep, encompassing the development of rich AI solutions for multiple organizations and their customers. This has given me exposure to various domains such as banking, insurance, healthcare, retail, and manufacturing.

In the competitive world of Kaggle, I'm a Triple Kaggle Grandmaster, a Competitions Master, a former rank #1 holder, a former rank #2 holder globally, and 5x Winner of Kaggle competitions.

Master's graduate from the National University of Singapore, where I received the honor of Valedictorian. My academic accolades also include winning the NUS Innovation Challenge, the Global Fintech Competition, and the Outstanding Capstone Award.
